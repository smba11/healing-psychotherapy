Welcome

Hello and welcome! I’m Tesfaye Gebreyohannes, PMHNP, and I’m glad you’re here. I believe therapy is a collaborative journey — a place where your story matters, your experience is honored, and your growth is the focus.

Whether you’re struggling with anxiety, mood concerns, stress, or simply feeling stuck, my goal is to create a safe, nonjudgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and goals at your own pace.

Therapy isn’t about having all the answers — it’s about uncovering your strengths, understanding your patterns, and learning new ways to respond to life’s challenges. I look forward to walking alongside you as you build a life that feels meaningful and authentic.

  • Anxiety and stress management
  • Depression and mood concerns
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Life transitions and identity exploration
  • Relationship and interpersonal issues
  • Self-esteem and personal growth
